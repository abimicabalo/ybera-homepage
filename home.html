About Surigao City

Surigao City is a coastal city in the northeastern region of Mindanao, Philippines. It is known for its breathtaking natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant local community. With its stunning beaches, captivating islands, and fascinating landmarks, Surigao City offers an unforgettable experience for travelers seeking adventure and relaxation.
